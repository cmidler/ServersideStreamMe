Personal info

whoYu only collects the information we need to provide the service our users want, and NOTHING else. When you sign up for whoYu, we collect your unique device identifier (UDID), and your zip code. This allows whoYu to ensure our service is released in areas our users want it and to allow our app to funtion properly. The vast majority of the information pulled from facebook, that the user can view/edit/remove from her/his profile, is stored locally on that user's device and is never sent to our company.

Children

whoYu is intended for use for adults of age 18 or older. Children should not be utilizing this application.

How we collect info

whoYu only collects a user's email address, unique device identifier (UDID) and zip code upon registration. Once connected with a thrid party social network, whoYu pulls pertinent profile information into the user's device's local cache. This is NOT sent to our company, but instead is intended to ease the effort of setup on our users. The limited amount of user data we do collect, is kept fully encrypted and secure throughout the process of sending, storing and deletion.

Tracking technologies

No tracking technologies are utilized by whoYu. whoYu is able to give users access to other users within 100ft of her/him by utilizing BTLE technology that comes from your device. GPS and all other location positioning systems are not necessary.

How we use your info

whoYu only stores the data that is necessary for a great user experience. Almost all data (except user's email address and UDID) are stored and broadcasted from that user's personal device, and is never sent to our company. We utilize the small amount of user data we collect to help first time users set up her/his profiles and to allow notifications from our app.
